Arctic Fox

Arctic fox fur changes seaonally. Fluffy white coat in the winter, browinsh or grayish in the summer

Scientific Name: Vulpes Lagopus
Length: Up to 110 centimeters
Life Span: 3-6 years
Habitant: Arctic and alphine tundra
Diet: Birds, Lemming, Squirrel
Mass: 3.2-9.4 kg (Male, Adult), 1.4-3.2 kg (Female, Adult)

A small fox well adopted to living in a cold environments, and is best known for its thick, warm fur that is also used as a camouflage.
